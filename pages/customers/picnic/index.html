Picnic case study

How Picnic uses Snowplow data to create a personalized shopping experience.

Picnic launched in 2015 as a mobile-first supermarket to make grocery shopping simple, fun and affordable for everyone. Picnic uses machine learning to power innovation and operations, and it is now the world’s fastest growing online supermarket.

Key results

  • Picnic enhanced user experience by using Snowplow data to create personalized product recommendations
  • Increased retention by using Snowplow's event-level data to predict when users were likely to churn
  • With Snowplow’s scalable infrastructure, Picnic easily adapted to growing data volumes without service interruptions

The challenge

Picnic’s customer base was growing by more than 500 percent year-over-year; Picnic needed a data infrastructure that could easily scale and evolve as its business grew. As a mobile-only supermarket, Picnic also wanted to understand each stage of their customers’ journey to deliver a customized and user-friendly experience.


The solution

Snowplow’s modular infrastructure and auto scaling technology allowed Picnic to easily adjust to rapidly growing data volumes. What’s more, using Snowplow’s granular, event-level data, Picnic could quickly A/B test new app features and deliver customized product recommendations to optimize the user experience.

“The Snowplow framework has become indispensable to understanding the Picnic app usage and allowing us to focus on the most impactful features.”

Dimitar Nedev
Data Infrastructure Lead at Picnic
