JustWatch case study

How JustWatch uses Snowplow data to provide highly personalized content and adverts.

Download case study

JustWatch is a streaming search engine where users can find the best place to watch their favorite movies and TV shows. In addition, JustWatch enables movie studios and Video on Demand providers to create effective and enjoyable advertising towards highly targeted audience segments.

Industry
AdTech

Download case study

Key results

  • JustWatch built their own anonymous identity management service on top of their Snowplow data to successfully identify users across channels, and target specific users across any channel
  • JustWatch used their Snowplow data to develop superior user intelligence and build profiles around 50 million movie fans
  • Using Snowplow’s data, JustWatch was able to optimize trailer delivery and double watch time compared to the industry average

The challenge

JustWatch serves targeted video content to consumers, while also allowing movie studios to advertise TV shows and movies to highly defined audience segments. To improve user experience and targeting, JustWatch needed access to detailed data about their user base so they could track activity across platforms and gain an understanding of users’ tastes in movies and TV shows. Then, JustWatch would be able to match users with customized trailers and TV show suggestions through sophisticated machine learning models, and increase the efficiency of movie studios’ trailer advertising campaigns.


The solution

JustWatch implemented Snowplow to collect rich, granular, event-level data across various channels, which made it possible for JustWatch to stitch together user journeys and deliver a single customer view. Then, on top of the advanced user intelligence gained through Snowplow data, JustWatch built an audience tech that enabled studios to advertise with an unprecedented level of control and precision, increasing return on ad spend. What’s more, JustWatch’s team of data scientists were able to power machine learning models and clustering algorithms with their Snowplow data to match users with targeted content.

“Snowplow’s rich, granular data enabled us to build sophisticated audience intelligence and double the efficiency of our clients’ trailer advertising campaigns.”

Dominik Raute
Co-Founder & CTO at JustWatch
