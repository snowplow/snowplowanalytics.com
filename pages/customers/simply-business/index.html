Simply Business is a leading online business insurance broker with over 475,000 customers. Simply Business is also known as a technology and data innovator placing data at the heart of everything they do; from product improvements to company strategy and direction.

Key results

  • A unified view of the customer journey enabled Simply Business to create a seamless cross-channel user experience
  • Optimized marketing using a multi-channel attribution model built on top of Snowplow data
  • Developed a personalized website experience using real-time behavioral data
  • Built an A/B testing framework for website updates to enable systematic, data-driven improvements
  • Improved board-level reporting of core metrics

The challenge

Simply Business engages with customers across disparate platforms and channels and the data team wanted to gain a holistic view of the user journey in order to provide better service at every touchpoint, optimize marketing spend and systematically grow the business. What’s more, Simply Business needed a way to process user data in real-time to improve the customers’ web and mobile experience, and keep call center staff better informed.


The solution

With Snowplow’s real-time data pipeline Simply Business was able to collect, process and store data from multiple data streams in a simple and scalable way. As a result, Simply Business gained a unified view of their customer journey, created a seamless cross-channel experience for their users, and optimized conversions with a multi-channel attribution model. What’s more, Snowplow’s data integration with Zendesk empowered the call center staff to improve customer service and ultimately close more deals.

Stewart Duncan
