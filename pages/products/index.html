The data landscape is complicated but the data value chain is simple.

Snowplow Insights Snowplow React

Data collection is the foundation of your data value chain.

In a multi-channel, multi-platform world, building a data set that accurately records history of all your interactions with all your users across all those platforms, in a format to enable you to build insight and intelligence, is a big job. Snowplow Insights is built to enable you to do just that. It gives you the data foundation so you can record and understand each one of your user’s complete user journeys.

Data is only as valuable as the decisions you make and actions you take with it.

There are countless channels, platforms and actions you might want to take in response to the data you’ve collected and intelligence you’ve developed on that data. Snowplow ReactBETA enables you to manage the myriad network of decisions and actions, across channels and platforms, in real-time, so you can provide a high quality, consistent and personalized experience for each of your users. .
